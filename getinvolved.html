Get Involved!

Studies show that kids today are not getting the caffeine that their growing bodies need. How can you help?

Donate

You can make a monetary donation here that will provide one cup of coffee to a child in need.

Sponser a Child for One Year

Contact us about sponsoring a child. Your gift of sponsorship will provide one cup of coffee each weekday to a child in need for one year. You will also receive a Kaffeinated Kids tote bag, as well as a monthly progress report with updates about your child.

Sponser a Classroom for One Year

Contact us about sponsoring a classroom. Your gift of sponsorship will provide one cup of coffee each weekday to a classroon of children for one year. You will also receive a Kaffeinated Kids tote bag, two Kaffeinated Kids coffee mugs, and a monthly progress report with updates about your classroom.
